Frequenty Asked Questions - FAQ

What are retailers, suppliers and vendors??

GTienda lets you create your own distribution network for your products. You can easily link retailers to your catalog to sell through them.

You can retail for networks created by other companies, your providers. You can use their catalogs to build yours

If you have a physical store, you will probably have sellers that help you with the sale. GTienda allows them to use your catalog for build orders in your store.

What is a product?

Is a reference or barcode. A product can have different colors and sizes.

Is there a charge for installation?

No. There are not cost by installation in any of our plans.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. At any time you can cancel or suspend your account from your control panel.

How long are your contracts?

All GTienda plans are month to month, unless you contract an annual or biennial plan.

Do I need to enter the details of my credit card to register?

No. You can sign up for the FREE version without entering the details of your credit card. For other plan you must have a PayPal account.

If you want to receive money from anywhere in the world, PayPal is a good solution.

What is PayPal and how it works?

PayPal is the payment system more known worldwide. Creating an account is easy and secure. With this account you can pay and collect worldwide.

After choosing a plan, the "Buy Now" button will take you to PayPal site. If you do not have an account, you can create one at that time. If you already have an account, you must login and approve payment.

When you come back to GTienda you must sign in with your email and password.

Do you offer any discounts on your plans?

Yes, we offer discounts of 10% on annual plans and 20% on biennial plans.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely, you can ascend or descend plans at any time.

What are your bandwidth fees?

None. All GTienda plans include free unlimited bandwidth.

Do I need web hosting?

No. All GTienda plans include accommodation for secure unlimited e-commerce for your store. GTienda uses the best servers, networks and a global CDN for ensure the availability and speed of your ecommerce site.
