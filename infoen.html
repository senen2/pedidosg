GTienda, how it works

Register

Add products

Product creation is done by adding pictures of them. You can do this in several ways:

  • Select images from your PC, tablet or phone
  • Take pictures and upload them immediately
  • Drag images from other sites

You can arrange the catalog images by drag and drop them.

Add data

Now you can move through the images to add necessary data to each product.

It is important to add tags or labels that serve to help customers find what they want or to facilitate the setting of prices.

Promote your site

You've got your website ready to sell.

You can now tell your clients and friends, by word of mouth or by email, posting on Facebook, Twitter... Maybe put some stickers at the entrance and inside of your business.

Many of yours clients seeing how simple it is to buy by your page could increase its purchases.

The more people know about your site, more likely they tell others and increase your orders.

Customers place orders

On the website, customers observe the product catalog and consult product details, use the filters (created with tags) and put what they want on the cart. In the cart they can review, remove or add products.

When they are ready they will place the order or request a quote. If you have a Paypal account they can paid at this time, otherwise you will tell them how make the payment.

You enter orders

You can also enter orders from your store or office, to serve yours customers as it has done so far.

Being inside your account, drag products from the catalog or write the reference. In this way you can also change the order.

Deliver on time

Don't make your client suffer because you forgot to delivery an order.

Your page has mechanisms to track the execution of orders.

Assign work

If you are a manufacturer or a third-party service integrator, you can have our complete production control system to assist you to assign work to different teams, control materials required.

Production control

At any time, easily establish the work excution order for each team.

This way you can optimize your production or make last minute changes without disturbing the production.
